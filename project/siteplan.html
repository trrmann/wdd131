Manual Encryption - Site Plan

Site Name

Manual Encryption

This name represents the content and purpose of the site in the most efficient manner.

Domain availability: manual-encryption.org

Logo image:

Site Purpose

The site provides the ability to perform manual encryption in three outdated forms that were taught by the United States Army for Signals Intelligence Analysts.

Scenarios

What are the encrypted and decrypted messages using scytale encryption, given an appropriate key.

What are the encrypted and decrypted messages using simple substitution encryption, given an appropriate key.

What are the encrypted and decrypted messages using a playfair encryption(5x5 matrex), given an appropriate key.
